AR10 HANDGUARDS - IN STOCK NOW

17”, 15”, and 13.5” are available. 2.160 OD and 2.00 ID with great .080" thick wall stiffness. M-Lok cuts all around just like our AR15/AR9 and MPX Competition Handguards. The 2.00 ID is large enough to accommodate a bull barrel and an SLR or Superlative adjustable gas block. 17” is for 18” barrels, 15” is for 16” barrels and the 13.5” is for 14.5” barrels. This will leave a little less than 1/2” of barrel showing before the muzzle brake. Barrel Nuts are 7075 Aluminum with form thread tapping and have mil-spec Type 3 Hardcoat. NOTE: You will need the Precision Reflex DPMS AR10 barrel nut wrench or similar tool to tighten barrel nut.
